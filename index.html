About the Doctor

A physician devoted to your heart & wellbeing

Dr. Ayesha Rahman completed her MBBS from Dhaka Medical College and earned her FCPS in Cardiology, followed by advanced fellowship training in Interventional Cardiology in Singapore. She has led the Cardiac Care Unit at Square Hospital for over a decade.

She believes healthcare should be personal, preventive, and compassionate — combining modern diagnostics with genuine listening.
