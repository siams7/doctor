Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Rahman Heart & Health Clinic ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and medical information in accordance with applicable healthcare data protection standards.

Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, contact us, or use our website, we may collect your name, email, phone number, date of birth, medical history, and appointment preferences.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to schedule appointments, provide medical care, send appointment reminders, and improve our services. We never sell your personal or medical data to third parties.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect patient records stored in our systems and Patient Portal.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences (such as dark mode) and for basic analytics. You may decline non-essential cookies at any time via the cookie banner.

Your Rights
